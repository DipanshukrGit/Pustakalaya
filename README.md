## 📖 About Pustakalaya

Pustakalaya is a full-stack mobile application designed for readers who love exploring and discussing books. The platform allows users to discover new books, write detailed reviews, rate books, and share their reading experiences with the community.

Built with a scalable architecture using React Native and Expo for the mobile application and Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB for the backend, Pustakalaya delivers a smooth and engaging experience across devices.

Whether you're a casual reader, a student, or a passionate book enthusiast, Pustakalaya helps you keep track of your reading journey while discovering recommendations from other users.

### ✨ Why Pustakalaya?

- Share your thoughts on books you've read
- Discover books recommended by the community
- Rate books and help others find quality reads
- Create your personal reading profile
- Upload book cover images with reviews
- Secure authentication and personalized experience
